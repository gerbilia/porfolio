About Me

I’m Gaby Santos, a Latine Writer and Creative Specialist from Miami, now based in New York. I graduated from the University of Miami with a degree in Film and Journalism and sharpened my writing skills in Los Angeles through freelance projects, including developing TV pilots, creating narrative podcasts, and building social media engagement strategies—like managing a meme page with over 130,000 followers. Currently, I’m pursuing a Master’s in Communications with a focus on Journalism Innovation at Syracuse University’s Newhouse School. My work explores the intersection of storytelling, technology, and social media, focusing on integrating new tech and digital strategies to drive meaningful engagement and industry impact.
